Incident Response

If you have or are experiencing a breach in your network security, don’t hesitate- call us now!

FalconFox can help you recover from a security incident and manage your situation with swift and effective measures that will limit the damage and reduce your potential loss.

Here’s our process:

  • Prepare

    • The first thing to do to help prevent and recover from an attack is to prepare you. We will help educate and train you on the security measures so that when you are the first one on the scene you can immediately deal with the situation.

  • Identify

    • We will identify the problem so that we can use the proper tools and knowledge to remove the problem.

  • Contain

    • We will assess how far the damage has spread and contain the event so that further damage cannot be done.

  • Eradicate

    • Investigations will take place to find the source of the problem and remove the malicious offender.

  • Recovery

    • Files, data and software will be recovered and restored from reliable and clean backups.

  • Documentation

    • The best way to prevent and prepare is to learn from the incident and improve and fix the measures that keep your network safe. We go through the what, why and how to keep it from happening again in the future.
