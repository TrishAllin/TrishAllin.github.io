Colorado Photo Expo

During my tenure at Mike's Camera, I created & edited the main website, digital & social media graphic content. I was fortunate enough to be there for the launch of their new Colorado Photo Expo, the first & only of its kind in Colorado. It would prove to be a large event & we decided it needed its own site to highlight vendors, presenters, event information & specials. I created the wireframes, mockups & basic code framework in a matter of a few weeks. It was put on hold, then the trigger was pulled & I was told they wanted it content & all live within a week! I gathered, wrote & coded all content, finalized design & met the deadline. I also provided maintenance as more content was added & edited. I created a new suite of icons, the expo logo & the graphics from scratch with a bold new look while maintaining the company's main brand integrity.
