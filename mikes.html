Mike's Camera

Based out of Boulder, Colorado, Mike's Camera is one of the largest camera, photography and imaging stores from Colorado to Calfornia. They purchased multiple Wolf Camera stores in the last few years to grow from 3 stores in Colorado to 13 across Colorado and California. I designed & created all in-store & exterior signage, all magazine, newspaper & print ads, vehicle graphics, email newsletter, digital & website graphics. Here's some of the larger projects I achieved while I was there.

Branding

Due to their fast growth, Mike's needed an immediate solution to ensure consistent branding for all of their stores and advertising- in-house and outsourced. I created their first Identity Branding Guidelines for in-house and outsourced design.
