The Denver Post is putting on this expo with Mike's Camera as the presenting sponsor.

About the Colorado Photo Expo

Shop and learn to your heart’s desire for all your photographic curiosities to make your photography, and travel photography more fun and rewarding. There will be two speaker stages with seating, where speakers and professionals will present free classes on various travel and photo related topics every 30 minutes from 10AM – 4:00PM.

You’ll be able to buy gear from the world’s leading photography manufacturers including Canon, Nikon, Sony, Tamron, Panasonic, Olympus, GoPro, Manfrotto, Promaster, and more will be available at special discounts at the event.

You’ll also find special deals on Mike’s Camera imaging services that include printing and various new output methods, and fun contests, swag & giveaways for attendees.

What will I find at the Colorado Photo Expo?

You’ll Discover Photography: the World’s Greatest Hobby… Accessible. Challenging. Fun!

You could read all about the latest equipment online in various blogs and website reviews. But the Colorado Photo Expo is the one place that will allow you the opportunity to experience first-hand all the latest products rather than simply reading about them. Come see, touch, and explore the latest gear and the surprisingly creative new accessories that will make your picture-taking more creative, fun and rewarding. Whether you’re a high-end D-SLR Camera user or can never put away your smart phone for your picture taking, you’ll be pleased to discover all that’s new to make your picture taking, picture making, picture sharing and picture editing more enjoyable! All our programs, from our keynote speakers to our vendor presentations, are designed to deepen and enhance your relationship with the entire photography experience -- from image capture, image editing and manipulation, to image output; whether in the form of conventional prints or creating unique photo-inspired creations.

About the Amazing Adventures Expo

Amazing Adventures Travel & Recreation Expo is an event for families and anyone who loves to travel! Admission is FREE and you’ll learn about all the wonderful things to do in Colorado and beyond. With over 100 national and international destinations, plus food samples, raffles and giveaways, the Expo has something for everyone. Visit booths about Colorado destinations, family vacation destinations, amusement parks, cruise lines, airlines and casinos. Learn how to plan your next trip, save money for vacations and how to take the best photographs. Plus you can explore booths about hiking, kayaking, bike riding, white water rafting, zip-lining, tubing, skiing, RV’s, ATV’s, motorcycle trips, destination cooking and painting classes and so much more!
