Experience

Welcome to The Colorado Photo Expo community – the place to turn people who enjoy taking pictures into people passionate about their picture taking!

Capture the photographer in you.



Education Theaters 1 & 2

Both our Theaters will feature information-rich and fun-filled sessions that will keep your attention. Our programs are intentionally fast-paced to give you the opportunity to participate in as many sessions as possible. All of our presentations at the Colorado Photo Expo are FREE, but seating is limited. These classes will also give you a taste of the many photography classes, seminars and workshops we conduct all year long. Our classes will help get better you familiarized with your camera, and also teach a variety of photographic techniques and genres, from wildlife, portrait, landscape and close-up photography to available light and flash use. Our past attendees have told us that they’ve found our classes both inspiring and empowering, and with those goals in mind, we look forward to sharing our expertise with you!

Photo Shooting Zone

The Photo Shooting Zone will provide opportunities for learning new photographic tips and tricks. With information shared by photo professionals, you'll walk away with added knowledge on lighting, posing, and numerous other facets of photography.

The Learning Center

The Learning Center offers participants an opportunity to explore the building blocks of photography. The principles of aperture and shutter speed come into clear focus with simple exercises. You will be guided in live photo setting to choose and use f-stop and shutter speed effectively.

Your Walls Your Pictures

If you have read this far, you probably have great photos – your own work, your kids’ professional school or sports photos, or precious family images handed down through generations. Now what? Don’t leave them languishing in your phone, camera or a drawer… get Your Photos on Your Walls!

Our custom imaging professionals and picture framers will show you options and solutions in choosing the perfect mat, molding and other design ideas – from simple frames to more elaborate custom shadow box frames for memorabilia, canvas wrap prints or output on gleaming metal– your pictures will make a wonderful addition for your home, office, or a gift for family and friends!

Creative Photo Products

Our Creative Photo Products Zone will amaze you. Here, you will discover so many things you didn’t know you could do with your pictures. Get your creative juices flowing! You will go home with the very latest, most innovative ideas for using your images, to decorate your home or someone else’s, make unforgettable gifts, design one-of-a-kind works of art, and share the memories you cherish with family and friends – all in ways you’ve never before imagined!

Get Social!

The Social Media Zone is a place especially for social media fans and members of the press. It’s where you can take a break from the action, and write about everything that’s happening. Share the great ideas and products you discover, the tricks and tips you learn, the experts and other attendees you meet. In the Social Media Zone, you can relax, write, and interact with others in a comfortable area. Like us on Facebook and stay in the loop with special offers and drawings from the expo!

Photography Exhibition

Do you love great photographs? You won't want to miss the photo exhibitions at Colorado Photo Expo! See amazing, inspiring images guaranteed to take your breath away. These incredible pictures, captured by a variety of photographers, will motivate you to get out and get behind the lens and take your own photography to the next level.

Exhibitors

Whether you are looking for cameras, photo equipment, accessories, output & printing solutions, assistance, advice, or ideas for the perfect gift for Fathers’ Day or a Graduate, you will be sure to find it among the Colorado Photo Expo exhibitors. This is your chance to not only find these items, but to talk one-on-one with the companies that make them.

Discover, touch, try and buy the latest tools, equipment and products to make your photographs better than you ever thought possible! The exhibitors will be offering a variety of specials all day long. Find out more here!
