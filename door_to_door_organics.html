Door to Door Organics

Door to Door Organics has been one of the true highlights of my career to date. I worked with a team front-end designer/developers, backend and software quality assurance engineers, an agile product owner and a product/UX director to take a small organic food company's website to the next level. When I came to DTDO, the team was beginning the underpinnings of turning the dated, non-responsive site mobile, accessibility friendly with a fresh and friendly UI.

I started out learning the new SASS and HTML framework, participating in the designs and wireframes for the new site, while helping maintain legacy content. Instead of choosing a waterfall rollout, I helped replaced dated content and design piece by piece from the site- much to the delight of our customers!

During the rollout of the new site, we realized that our framework was simply just not robust enough and started to implement a React/Nuclear core to the site. I was instrumental in user testing and developing small components in this switch over. It was a joy to be a part of not only the designing, but the devolpment of such a large conversion, particularly the fun, engaging subject matter and customer experience
