a little bit about me...

Hello there, friend! I'm Trish & I am a graphic designer & UX Engineer/Front End Developer with over 15 years of combined experience. I'm your quintessential Colorado girl living in the foothills of the Front Range & there's no where else I'd rather be!

I am the mother of two small children who married her college sweetheart & our family loves to go on adventures! We are private rafters that love to float the river on our big purple raft. My husband & I have rafted over ten rivers including the Colorado, Gunnison, Upper Taylor, Lake Fork, Dolores, San Juan, Desolation Canyon, the Headwaters of the Rio Grande & the Yampa. Our kids have been rafting since they were three years old! We hike, love to camp, & you can often find us riding our bikes on the Greenway- you'd know me anywhere by my Hello Kitty townie with hot pink flames!

I've been playing guitar since I was ten, & my 12 string Ovation is my third baby. I love music of all kinds as evident by my vinyl collection which looks like it could belong to about five different people. I'm a fun & positive person with a large base of useless trivia knowledge, an uncanny ability to name that tune & recite song lyrics to music that was way before my time. I have a passion for animation, my favorites are Genndy Tartakovsky, Craig McCracken, Disney & Pixar. Being a leap year baby, that means my age is still in the single digits, giving me a great excuse to never have to grow up!

Thanks for visiting, I hope you like what you see... feel free to contact me through LinkedIn if you'd like me to bring my skills & talents your way!
