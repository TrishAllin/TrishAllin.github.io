Event Posters & Magazines

Boil & Boogie

I created the posters and advertising for the Mead Boil and Boogie festival two years in a row. These were some of my favorite projects because they gave me complete creative freedom!

Niwot Nostalgia Days

For four years I was in charge of the advertising and poster design for the Niwot Nostalgia Day festival in Niwot, Colorado. This was one of the projects I looked forward to every year- I got to re-design it based on a pre-determined theme for the year. One of the biggest challenges was working with a group of people who all had a say and opinion on it and working with a lack of actual photos from the festival.

Longmont Magazine

Longmont Magazine was the flagship product for the Times-Call. I was asked to re-brand it for it's 15 year anniversary. I recreated and updated the Longmont Magazine masthead/logo for all print and online publications. A professional photographer took the cover shots and I was in charge of designing, helping with captions, photo correction and layout for three years.

Holiday Gift Guide

In 2008 and 2011 I designed the Holiday Gifting Magazine covers. These set the tone for the entire selling and promotional campaigns.
