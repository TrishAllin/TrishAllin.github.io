Welcome to Summit County, Colorado!

The old mining town near Breckenridge

Nestled on the Western Slope of the Rocky Mountains, you should find yourself in Summit County, Colorado. Whether you are looking to warm yourself by a campfire, hit the slopes, relax in a hot tub under starry mountain sky or do some world class shopping and skiing, we've got it all.
