Featured Event for September

Crystal Lake Climb
Sept. 15, 2012

Join us on a spectacular 4x4 adventure up to the Crystal Lakes between Peak 10 and Mount Helen. We'll have a relaxing mountain afternoon at Lower Crystal Lake at the end of the jeep trail or you can hike up to the Upper Crystal Lake or Continental Falls. The hike is highly recommended with clear trails, creeks, cabins and mines, but we won't blame you if you just want to relax and take in the scenery. Call for reservations.

Upcoming Events and Excursions

Sept. 22, 2012 Family Fun Day

Alpine slides, hiking and biking all afternoon with a full BBQ dinner- burgers, brisket, hot dogs, baked beans and all the fixin's! End the day with your family or loved one around a campfire making s'mores and drinking hot chocolate with a real country guitarist. Don't worry, you only have to sing along if you want to! Reservations required.

Oct. 20, 2012 Drive the Divide

Bring your sense of adventure and love for open space and take a 4x4 Jeep tour across the Continental Divide to the top of Georgia Pass to the Glacier Ridge trailhead. Never have you seen vistas like this or had so much fun getting there! Picnic lunch served at the summit. Reservations required.

First Ski

We're taking you to ski the first day of the season. Fresh powder, sun and outdoor fun will be our way of welcoming in the Colorado winter season! How could you miss opening day? Let us make it easy and fun for you. Lift ticket, equipment rental and lunch provided. Lessons extra. Reservations required.
