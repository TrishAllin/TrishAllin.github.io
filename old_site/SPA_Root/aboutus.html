About Summit Peak Adventures

Greg and Trisha Allin moved to Summit County in 2001 to follow their dreams and their love for skiing and make it a full time adventure. They started as independent ski and snowmobile guides, but their clientele quickly grew and their reputation for providing great outdoor experiences spread and by the winter of 2003 they started their own company Summit Peak Adventures.

Their extended family saw how much fun and success they were having and moved down the road from them and decided to become a part of the business. Greg's father, Ron, also an expert skier helped out with their guided ski tours while Trisha's father, Craig, and brother, Jason, took their love and expertise in off-road four-wheeling and brought a whole new dimension to the Summit Peak Adventures business. Their friends Brian and Kara joined the business soon after with their established horse ranch and added sleigh rides to the experience.
