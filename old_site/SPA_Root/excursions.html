Featured Event
for September

Crystal Lake Climb
Sept. 15, 2012

Join us on a spectacular 4x4 adventure up to the Crystal Lakes between Peak 10 and Mount Helen. We'll have a relaxing mountain afternoon at Lower Crystal Lake at the end of the jeep trail or you can hike up to the Upper Crystal Lake or Continental Falls. The hike is highly recommended with clear cut trails, creeks, old cabins and mines, but we won't blame you if you just want to relax and take in the scenery. Call for reservations.

Excursions and Adventures

  • Ski & Snowboard Packages

    • Never been skiing? Shred master? Doesn't matter- we've got the package for you. Whether you need everything from rental equipment and some lessons on how to get down the hill or whether you're ready to go off trail on CAT guided day of boarding the backcountry, we have the equipment, certified instructors and experienced guides to make your day a snowy adventure!

  • Snow Biking

    • Not ready for the planks or board but still want to cruise the slopes? Have a seat on our easy to learn, easy to ride snow bikes and have the same experience as a skier or snowboarder. We'll help you get your snow biking license and you'll be flying down the mountain in no time!

  • Jeep Ride

    • Want to see Summit County the way you can on our snowmobile tours, but missing the snow? No problem! We'll take you off the pavement on a 4x4 tour of our extensive road and trail networks that can get your adrenalin pumping or your peaceful mountain serenity on. Guided or unguided, it's a memorable day for the whole family!
Off-roading the Backcountry

  • Sleigh Rides

    • Join us for a good, old-fashioned ride in our authentic sleigh drawn by our beautiful Clydesdale horses. Tour the town and surrounding area by day and snuggle up under the blanket with your sweetheart for a ride under the stars complete with warm mugs of hot cocoa. The only way to end a day in our amazing countryside.

  • Alpine Slides

    • The slide isn't just for kids anymore! Ride the chairlift to the top and cruise almost a mile down our alpine slides. Race on down or enjoy the ride on our self-controlled sleds for ages 3 to 300. It's fun for everyone and a race to the finish line!
Summit Peak Ski School

  • Guided Snowmobile

    • See Colorado backcountry like you've never seen it before! Cruising the trails and the unbelievable views from the Continental Divide on our state-of-the-art snowmobiles will convince you to never go home. Our experienced, knowledgeable and personable guides are happy to take you solo or the whole family, not to mention it's a great time for company events.

  • Snowshoeing

    • If you are looking for a more peaceful trip through the Summit County wilderness than the roar of snowmobiles and the wind in your hair, then we've got guided snowshoe tours that are right up your alley! The fresh air, quiet of nature, wildlife and beautiful scenery with just the crunch of the snow beneath your feet on the miles of our trails will set you up for nice night snuggled up by the fire with your sweetheart.
