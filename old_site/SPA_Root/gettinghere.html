Getting Here

While we are located in the heart of the Summit County wilderness, we're not that hard to find! Take a scenic drive off of I-70 past Dillon Reservoir through the town of Breckenridge to our scenic ranch site. Visit us today!

Directions: From Interstate 70 take exit 203 and head south on Highway 9/North Summit Blvd. Take Highway 9 through the heart of Breckenridge about 12 miles to just past Goose Pasture Tarn Lake. Head East on Timberline Trail. 5 miles down the dirt road and you're here!
