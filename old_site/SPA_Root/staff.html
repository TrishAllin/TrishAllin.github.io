Meet Our Staff

  At Summit Peak Adventures our staff is family- literally! We invite you to check out the faces and learn a little bit more about who will help give you the adventure of a lifetime.

Greg Allin
Owner/Ski & Snowboard Expert
Owner of Summit Peak, Greg is living his dream of skiing every day. He loves to share his passion for the slopes with every guest that's ready for the adventure.
Trisha Allin
Owner/Ski Expert
The other half of Summit Peak, there's nothing Trisha loves more than skiing and experiencing the beauty of their Summit County home.
Ron Allin
Ski Expert/Instructor
With over 50 years of skiing and ski patrol under his belt, Ron's a master instructor and the one you want by your side when you are carving turns on the slopes.
Craig Mullins
Ski and Off-road Expert
Craig's got over 30 years of mechanic and off-road vehicle experience. He makes sure that all of our guests have an exhilarating and safe time climbing the trails.
Joyce Allin
Property Manager/Front Desk
The first and kindest face you'll ever see here, Joyce takes care of all of our guests with loving care. If you need anything, she'll find a way to get it for you.
Shelley Mullins
Accounts Manager
Shelley, Trisha's mom, is the account manager and does the financial background work to help us keep the business going. She's the brains of the operation!
Brian Dorsey
Grounds Manager
There's nothing Brian loves more than being with our guests and he'll make sure the fire's going and give you expert fishing advice- no one knows the area better!
Kara Dorsey
Stable Manager
Kara has been around horses all of her life. She loves taking our clients on snowy sleigh rides and horseback rides up the trails into our beautiful backcountry.
Jason Mullins
Off-road Expert
Trisha's brother Jason is an experienced mechanic and off-road enthusiast for over 20 years. His expertise and fearless need for adventure will be sure to give you the off-road adventure of your life!
