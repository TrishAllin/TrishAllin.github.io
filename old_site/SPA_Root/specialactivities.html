Summit Peak Adventure Special Activities

Our Sleigh Horses Frosty and Chocolate

 

We have some great activites and things to do right here on site including fishing, hiking, biking and tent sites. There's campfires for roasting smores, and fun things to do for the entire family! We also have the largest alpine slides in the country for summer fun and romantic horse drawn sleigh rides in the winter.
