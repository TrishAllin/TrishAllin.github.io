Summit Peak Adventure Packages

Whether you want a one day excursion or a weekend long adventure, we've got the package that will give you the great time you're looking for! Just because it's not listed here, doesn't mean we can't or won't do it. Call us for reservations, more information, and customized packaging.

 

Ski and Snowboard Packages
BIKE THE SLOPES Try something new and learn to snow bike! One day of biking at Copper Mountain, Keystone, Breckenridge and Arapahoe Basin Includes a full day lift ticket, snow bike rental and licensing/lessons from one of our professional, certified instructors Recommended for ages 10 and up $150 Per Person
THE GREEN RUN One day of fun on the mountain! Choose between Copper Mountain, Keystone, Breckenridge and Arapahoe Basin Includes a full day lift ticket, equipment rental (skis, snowboard, boots and poles) and lessons from one of our professional, certified instructors Recommended for ages 5 and up $150 Per Person
THE BLUE RUN A 2 day adventure with an overnight at the resort of your choice! Choose between Copper Mountain, Keystone, Breckenridge and Arapahoe Basin Includes 2 full day lift tickets, equipment rental (snowbike, skis, snowboard, boots and poles) with one day of lessons from one of our professional, certified instructors Recommended for ages 5 and up $300 Per Person
THE BLACK DIAMOND A 3 day adventure with two overnight stays at the resort of your choice! Choose between Copper Mountain, Keystone, Breckenridge and Arapahoe Basin Includes 3 full day lift tickets, equipment rental (snowbike, skis, snowboard, boots and poles) and a one day snowcat ride to get some off-trail fresh tracks! Recommended for experienced skiiers only ages 16 and up $500 Per Person

 

jeep tour Packages
Come with our professional drivers and guides and enjoy the Summit County backcountry. Wildlife, local history and an outdoor adventure are waiting for you! All tours include beverages, gourmet snack, blanket, and raingear. Up to 6 people per vehicle.
Adults $70 • Children 12 and under $45
TRIPLE PLAY Take an all day trip up three trails- Swan River, Saints John and SOB Hill jeep trails. Drive along rolling rivers and see abandoned cabins.
SANTA FE PEAK A half day trip to the top of the mountain. Abandoned mines and great views are just some of the fun features of this trip that might have you seeing snow in the middle of summer
PEAK 10 A full day ride up to the top of Peak 10 with unbeatable vistas and a tour of the Briar Rose Mine. A narrow rocky trail provides a fun time for all.
