The photographer’s tools…

While I started out with a box camera, I changed to digital photography around 2005. Most images are shot with Nikon cameras. Currently I am using the D80 and D5000. For the “quickie” shots I use a Nikon point-and-shoot, and for the times I am rarely unprepared for an amazing photo moment I use my iPhone.
