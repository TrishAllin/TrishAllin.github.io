Animals...

Sometimes the easiest, and sometimes the most complicated models a photographer can have. They have their own subtle show of emotions that can be hard to capture, unlike humans that can take direction and play emotion that they may not necessarily feel. Happening upon them in their own habitats and capturing them in their natural states allow their inner beauty and emotions to shine through.
