Plants...

One of the most overlooked subjects of our daily life are the small bits of nature that make up the larger view of our environment. I try to bring the vibrant colors, complex shapes and implied motions up to a visual level where they become the focus of the surroundings. Seeing them in this light is a constant reminder to not forget the importance of even the littlest things.
