Landscapes...

A photographer’s life is inherently active, and I am no exception. I love the outdoors and I am constantly finding places to hike and explore- never without my camera. Ansel Adams has said about landscape photography,
“…there are at least two people in every picture – the photographer and the viewer." Peaceful, sad, angry, excited, radiant- emotions and feelings of people and they can all be found in nature. My goal is to just be there to capture it.
