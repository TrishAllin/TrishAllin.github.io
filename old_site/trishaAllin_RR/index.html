Welcome to Richard Reynolds Photography…

I started shooting photography in the early 1960’s as a young boy living in Alaska. I shot my first picture of Mt. McKinley with a simple box camera, and that was the start of a lifelong passion and direction.

Though the technology has evolved and the scenery has changed, and I have grown with it, one constant remains- my love to share my view of the world with others. I hope you enjoy it.
