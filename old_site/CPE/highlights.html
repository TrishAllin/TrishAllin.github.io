Highlights

The expo is going to be so full of exciting things to experience and see, we want to break it down and give you a head's up on our favorite events! From amazing keynote speakers, in-expo events, to fun new products and special post-expo workshops, there's so much to get excited about!

Keynote Speakers

We're beyond excited to bring to you some of the biggest names in photography and some of the best in their field! Get travel tips and tricks from names like Rick Sammons, Andy Katz, Rick Steves and many others who have traveled the globe and know what it takes to capture the right moments at the right times.

Trade-In & Trade-Up Event

Looking to upgrade your film or digital camera or DSLR? Check out our Trade-In Event at the Colorado Photo Expo and get top dollar for your gear!

10% Bonus on Trade-Ins During Colorado Photo Expo



If your current photo gear isn’t cutting it, or you’re ready to upgrade to a newer model, we’ll take your clean used equipment in trade, and we’ll give you a store credit to use towards your new purchase. Additionally for one day only, you’ll be able to take advantage of a 10% bonus on the trade-in value of your gear when you apply your trade towards the purchase of new gear. Take advantage of this one-day-only Special! Note: Many variables may affect the value given for any photo hardware. The trade-in value may increase or decrease depending on the condition of the item. Our on-site camera buyer will strive to give you top dollar for your gear.

Passport Photos

A passport is one thing a world-traveler can't do without. As a part of the Expo Experience to prepare you for your travels, our professional passport photographers will be on staff to take your passport photos and have them printed WHILE YOU WAIT! They are 100% guaranteed and you check one more thing off of your list!

The Camera Doctor

Just in time for summer vacation! Get your sensor and camera exterior cleaned while you wait at the Expo! Your sensors should be cleaned once a year, and depending on the shooting conditions you work or play in, maybe more! Let our experts take care of you special equipment at a savings from the everyday cost!

SAVE 20% on The Learning Center

Save 20% on all Mike’s Camera Photography School Programs one day at the Expo only! Mike’s Camera offers a variety of programs, including Classes, Seminars and Workshops as closely related, yet distinctly different programs. We’re committed to helping you learn how to use your camera and take great looking photos that you can love for a lifetime!

Our Classes Include but are certainly not limited to:

• Basic & Advanced DSLR • Compact Camera • Wildlife Photography • Macro Photography • Studio Lighting and Speedlights • Travel Photography • Special Events, Guest Speakers & So Much More!
