Mike's Camera, Inc.

Boulder, CO • August 2013 to Present

Graphic Designer/Merchandise Promotions Coordinator

As the sole graphic designer for a chain of thirteen camera, photography and imaging stores, I am solely responsible for all advertising and branding creation, coordination and production.

  • Brand Identity...
    I created an Identity Branding Manual to ensure consistency and accuracy of the Mike’s Camera brand across thirteen stores and outsourced material in how it is portrayed in all aspects. Creation of new branding for services products.
  • Ad Design...
    Creating unique and eye-catching newspaper and magazine advertising for all services while adhering to branding standards, co-op regulations and the aesthetic preferences of the Chief Operating Officer.
  • Large Format Signage...
    Creating seasonal and semi-permanent store window signage in various formats and sizes according the thirteen different store window requirements, in formats appropriate for the store’s location and necessities (vinyl, paper, backlit material, etc). Continuous revamping and updating of various in-store signage including: store directories, menu boards, and service promotions.
  • Social Media Designs...
    The design and creation of in-post and cover graphics for Facebook and Email Blasts.
  • Web Design and Management...
    The creation of the Colorado Photo Expo website front to back end, continuously updating and ensuring all serices, banners and artwork for the Mike’s Camera website are current and accurate as well as working on a re-design of the “skin” of the site as a whole.
  • Packaging and Label Creation...
    Creating labels and packaging for Mike’s Camera’s unique line of products and services that esthetically refelct their purpose, necessities and uses.
  • Product Display Signage...
    Creating graphic and brand consistent display signage for Mike’s Camera brand and supported manufacturers products and services.
  • Tradeshow Graphics and Signage...
    Designing all promotional materials for all of Mike’s Camera events including rack cards, sandwich boards, window signage, tradeshow boots and all grid wall and tabletop displays.

Boulder, CO • January 2006 to May 2012

Asst Production Coordinator/Senior Designer

  • File and Information Organization and Backup.

  • Yellow Page and White Page Template Creation...
    A template for all listing and ad type sizes and leadings including overall page design.
  • Creation of Go To Guide Advertising...

  • Accurate Data Entry...

  • Research, Copy Writing and Editing...
    Full development of the “about our community” sections in all four Smart Books.
  • Pre-Flight and File Shipping...
    Responsible for compiling and sending completed files to be sent off to respective printing houses.
