Casey's Space Adventure

This is my first attempt at a children's learning app. Casey's Space Adventure's main character is named & modeled after my space fanatic son (he'll tell you he doesn't want to be an astronaut though- he wants to be a NASA engineer). It was a ton of research & learning about not just the solar system but ActionScript as well.
There is a page for each planet plus the sun, black holes, asteroids, blast off, landing & the solar system as a whole. Almost every page has multiple "play points"- learn about the planet, take a picture & put it in your scrapbook, read part of Casey's story & play a game.
